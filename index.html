About me

 I am PhD Student in Applied Mathematics ("MASSC"), my primary interest is in Machine Learning. I work with Nicolò Cesa-Bianchi and Claudio Gentile.

Summer 2012 -- I have been intern at Telefonica I+D (Barcelona), working with Alexandros Karatzoglou and Linas Baltrunas.

I obtained my M.Sc. in Computer Science from "Università degli studi di Milano" and I worked on "SPAN : Scalable Prediction Algorithms for Networked Data", under the supervision of prof. Nicolò Cesa-Bianchi.

Some of my jokes have been selected for this book.
